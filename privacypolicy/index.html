Lunar Fortune Privacy Policy

Log Files

Lunar Fortune follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they use the app. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used for analyzing trends, administering the app, tracking users' movements on the app, and gathering demographic information.

Privacy Policies of Advertising Partners

You may consult this list to find the Privacy Policy for each of the advertising partners of Lunar Fortune.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks may use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Lunar Fortune. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on this app or other apps or websites.

Please note that Lunar Fortune has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Lunar Fortune Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

Lunar Fortune does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our app, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
